\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a binary number and provides utilities for conversion, mutation, crossover, and manipulation. 

A \doxylink{classBinaryNumber}{Binary\+Number} stores its value as a string of \textquotesingle{}0\textquotesingle{} and \textquotesingle{}1\textquotesingle{} characters, and provides helper functions to convert between decimal and binary, perform crossover operations, mutate bits, and split into segments.
