\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an individual in a genetic algorithm population. 

An \doxylink{classIndividual}{Individual} is essentially a vector of \doxylink{classBinaryNumber}{Binary\+Number} objects, representing a candidate solution. It stores its fitness, rank, split locations, and other auxiliary fitness measures.
